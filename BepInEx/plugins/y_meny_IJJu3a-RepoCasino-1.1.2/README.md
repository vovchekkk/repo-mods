# 🚗 Description

![casino](https://i.imgur.com/hyFkoDm.jpeg)

This mod brings classic casino games like 🎰 Roulette and 🃏 Blackjack to the table!  
All players can bet their upgrades or in-game currency — no sitting out!  
It's all about luck. Pure luck. Can you feel it? 🎲

You can't bet money or upgrades if you don't have them.  
All upgrades placed by a player are tied to that player only — no borrowing your friend's upgrades and losing them by accident. 🚫🫂

To place a bet, first select what you want to bet (selection is local — 🟠 orange means selected, ⚪ white means not). Then click the bet area to place your wager. If you want to bet multiple times, just click multiple times. 💰🪙

All placed bets, drawn cards, and results are logged on TVs 📺 (one near the Roulette table, one near the Blackjack table).

# 🎰 ROULETTE

A classic Roulette experience. 🎲

Payout for a straight-up bet on a single number is **10:1** — if you bet 1 upgrade and win, you gain 10. If you lose, you lose 1. ❌🪙

Payout for betting on a third of the numbers (like Dozen or Column) is **2:2** — you win 2x or lose 2x. 🟢🔴

Payout for betting on half of the numbers (Red/Black, Even/Odd, 1-18/19-36) is **1:3** — you win 1x, but lose 3x. 🎯💸

Loss multiplier for money is always **1x** — no matter what kind of bet you make, if you lose, you lose exactly what you bet. 💸

![roulette](https://i.imgur.com/E2GZYI5.jpeg)

# 🃏 BLACKJACK

A simplified, fast-paced Blackjack variant. 🎯

No insurance, no splits — just hit, stand, and double down if you're feeling lucky. 🚫🃏

Payout multiplier is always **1:2** — bet 1 upgrade, win 1, lose 2. It's risky, but so is gambling! 🧨
